- Phone access

## Step 7: Reviews plan

After every successful repair:

1. Send the customer the Google review link.
2. Ask for honest feedback.
3. Mention the city and service naturally, for example:
   "If the roller repair helped, mentioning the city and what we fixed helps other homeowners find us."

Never buy fake reviews.

## Step 8: Weekly profile maintenance

Every week:

- Add 2-5 real photos.
- Publish one short update.
- Answer every review.
- Check messages and calls.
- Keep hours and service areas current.
