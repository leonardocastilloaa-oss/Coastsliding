# Facebook and Instagram campaign plan for CoastSlide

Goal: generate repair leads through calls, WhatsApp messages and form submissions.

## Campaign 1: Emergency / high-intent leads

Objective:

- Leads or Engagement with messages, depending on account options.

Budget:

- Start with $25-$50/day for 14 days.

Location:

- Miami-Dade, Broward and Palm Beach.
- Start with the counties, then split winners by region after data comes in.

Audience:

- Homeowners
- Condo owners
- Property managers
- HOA board members
- Ages 30+

Placements:

- Use Advantage+ placements first.
- Make sure Facebook Feed, Instagram Feed, Stories and Reels are included.

Creative:

- Vertical 9:16 video or before/after image.
- Show a stuck sliding door becoming smooth.
- Add captions because many people watch muted.

Primary text options:

1. Sliding door hard to open? Do not force it. Worn rollers can damage the track and turn a simple repair into a bigger job. CoastSlide repairs rollers, tracks, locks, windows and impact glass across South Florida. Call or message for a free estimate.

2. If your patio door grinds, sticks or will not lock, we can help. CoastSlide serves Miami-Dade, Broward and Palm Beach with sliding door and window repair, including rollers, tracks, locks and impact glass.

3. Property managers and HOA boards: repeated balcony door complaints usually mean a building-wide roller, track or lock pattern. CoastSlide provides multi-unit service and clear repair notes.

Headlines:

- Sliding Door Repair Today
- Door Stuck or Hard to Open?
- Roller, Track & Lock Repair
- South Florida Door Repair

Call to action:

- Call Now
- Send WhatsApp Message
- Get Quote

## Campaign 2: Retargeting

Objective:

- Leads or Traffic.

Audience:

- Website visitors from the last 30-180 days.
- Facebook and Instagram engagers.
- Video viewers.

Budget:

- $5-$15/day.

Creative:

- Reviews
- Before/after repairs
- Warranty/parts message
- Reminder before storm season

Text:

Still dealing with that heavy sliding door? CoastSlide can inspect rollers, tracks, locks and glass in one visit. Book a free estimate before the track gets damaged.

## Campaign 3: HOA and commercial

Objective:

- Leads.

Audience:

- Property managers
- Real estate managers
- Facility managers
- HOA interests
- Condo association interests

Creative:

- Technician/service image
- Condo balcony door repair
- Clean invoice / maintenance program angle

Text:

Managing repeated sliding door complaints? CoastSlide helps South Florida HOAs, condos and property managers with roller replacement, track repair, lock alignment, impact glass and quarterly maintenance programs.

## Creative checklist

- Use real repair photos.
- Use vertical format for Reels and Stories.
- Add captions to video.
- Show the problem in the first 2 seconds.
- Show the result clearly.
- Keep text simple and local.

## First 14-day testing plan

Days 1-3:

- Launch 3 ad creatives.
- Do not edit too quickly.

Days 4-7:

- Pause the worst creative if it spends with no leads.
- Keep the best 1-2 running.

Days 8-14:

- Duplicate winning creative.
- Test one new hook:
  - "Hard to open?"
  - "Before storm season"
  - "Condo balcony door complaints"

Metrics to watch:

- Cost per lead
- Cost per message
- Call clicks
- Landing page views
- Lead quality

## Important

Do not promise unrealistic same-day availability in every ad unless the schedule can support it. Use "same-day available" or "fast local service" instead.
