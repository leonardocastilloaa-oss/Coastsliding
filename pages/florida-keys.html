Why Doors and Windows Fail Differently in Florida Keys

The Florida Keys are a different category of corrosion. Standard hardware that survives inland can seize, pit or stain quickly in ocean air. Repairs here need to be planned around durable parts, efficient scheduling and prevention, especially for vacation homes that sit closed for long stretches.

Keys homes need marine-grade thinking: rollers, locks, screws, tracks, screens and window hardware should be evaluated as a system.

If a door is already hard to move before a long vacancy, repair it before leaving the property closed in salt-heavy air.

Repair Scenarios We See in Florida Keys

The heavy balcony slider

The door still moves, but only with two hands. This is usually the moment to inspect rollers before the track is scored.

The lock that suddenly stopped working

Often the lock is not the first failure. Worn rollers lower the panel and the hook no longer meets the keeper cleanly.

The pre-storm glass concern

Cracked or fogged impact glass should be evaluated before hurricane season so owners have time to plan.
